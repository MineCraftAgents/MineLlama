stick: You can craft stick from 2 planks.
beef: You have to kill cow to get beef. You don't need sword to kill it.
cobblestone: You need wooden_pickaxe to mine cobblestone.
cooked_beef: You have to smelt beef with furnace.
log: You can mine log without any tool.
planks: You can craft planks from log.
raw_iron: You need a stone_pickaxe to mine raw_iron.
iron_ingot: You can get iron_ingot by smelting raw_iron with furnace.
furnace: You can craft furnace with crafting table.
white_bed: You can craft white_bed with crafting table.
white_wool: You have to kill sheep to get white_wool.
crafting_table: You can craft crafting_table from 4 planks.
stone_pickaxe: You can craft stone_pickaxe with crafting_table.
iron_pickaxe: You can craft iron_pickaxe with crafting_table.
wooden_pickaxe: You can craft wooden_pickaxe with crafting_table.
